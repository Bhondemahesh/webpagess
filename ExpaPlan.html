Expansion Plan

  1. Creation of video conference facilities at Pune, Nashik, Sangli and Solapur for providing guidance to the grape growers on various aspect of viticulture.
  2. Recognition as post graduate teaching center in collaboration with Mahatma Phule Krishi Vidyapeeth (MPKV) Rahuri.
  3. Establishment of chain of Automatic Weather Stations in grape bid4essay growing areas of Maharashtra.
  4. Collaborative Research Programme with other renowned institutes.
  5. Appointment of technical staff for strengthening the research activities.
  6. Import of new promising varieties and supply the seedlings to the grape growers.
  7. Introduction of mechanization in viticulture.
